About StayTonga

StayTonga is more than a website — it’s a cultural gateway for travelers who want to experience the real Tonga.

Founded by local students with a passion for digital storytelling and cultural preservation, StayTonga helps visitors find authentic accommodations while supporting local families, eco-tourism, and sustainable travel.

Whether you're here to relax, explore, volunteer, or reconnect with your roots, we want you to feel at home on our islands — and in our hearts.

🌺 Our Mission

  • 🛖 Promote culturally respectful travel
  • 🌊 Support eco-friendly accommodation options
  • 💼 Empower young Tongans through digital innovation

🌏 Where We Are

StayTonga serves guests across Tongatapu, Vava‘u, Ha‘apai, and ‘Eua. We’re proud to connect the beauty of our islands with the world.
