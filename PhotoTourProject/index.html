Tongatapu is the largest island in Tonga and home to the capital city, Nuku'alofa. It's rich in cultural heritage and historical landmarks, including the famous Haʻamonga ʻa Maui trilithon. With its welcoming people and beautiful coastal views, Tongatapu is a must-visit destination.
